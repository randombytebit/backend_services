Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Remote Reproducibility  
Description: Promote the achievement of remote reproducibility of research results through portable source code accompanying published analyses, coupled with data, enabling verification, replication, and extension of computational results in CBB.  
Citation: Gentleman et al. (2004). Bioconductor: open software development for computational biology and bioinformatics. Genome Biology 2004, 5:R80. Page 1 (Abstract), Page 2 (Background), Page 7-8 (Reproducible research).  
Related Title: Microarray Gene Expression Analysis Pipelines, High-Throughput Sequencing Workflows, Clinical Trial Genomic Studies  

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-02  
Title: Transparency  
Description: Ensure transparency in high-throughput CBB pipelines by exposing the entire process from low-level data (e.g., microarray images) to statistical databases, allowing assessment of sensitivity to variations or errors.  
Citation: Gentleman et al. (2004). Bioconductor: open software development for computational biology and bioinformatics. Genome Biology 2004, 5:R80. Page 2 (Background).  
Related Title: Proteomics Data Processing Systems, Next-Generation Sequencing Pipelines, Drug Discovery Assay Platforms
