**Title:** Brute-Force Attack Protection

**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-03  
**Title:** Replay Attack Prevention  
**Description:** The system must prevent replay attacks by generating a new random challenge for each Passkey authentication attempt, combined with timestamped signatures, and by updating credential counters during login to ensure responses cannot be reused. [cite: Section III.A, Section IV.C.5]
