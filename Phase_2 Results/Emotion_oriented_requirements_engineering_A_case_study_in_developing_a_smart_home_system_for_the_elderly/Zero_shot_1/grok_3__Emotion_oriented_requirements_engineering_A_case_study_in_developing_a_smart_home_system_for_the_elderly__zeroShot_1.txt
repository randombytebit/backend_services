### Functional Requirements

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-01
  - **Requirement Title**: Support Independent Living
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must support independent living for elderly users by providing functionalities that allow them to live autonomously without constant human intervention.
  - **Citation**: "The goal of this system is to provide reminders (hydration, medication), identify potential security risks (back door has been left open), identify anomalous situations and to automate the physical environment (heating, cooling)." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Features

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-02
  - **Requirement Title**: Issue User-Specific Reminders
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must issue user-specific reminders for appointments, special events, hydration, and medication, triggered by user activity.
  - **Citation**: "The system functions include, issuing user specific reminders such as appointments and special events, hydration and medication reminders that can be setup to be triggered by user activity." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Features

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-03
  - **Requirement Title**: Detect Anomalous Behavior
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must detect anomalous behavior, such as a user spending an abnormal amount of time in bed, and issue alerts accordingly.
  - **Citation**: "Issuing alerts when anomalous behaviour is detected (eg: user is spending an abnormal time in bed)." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Features

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-04
  - **Requirement Title**: Provide Carer UI for Monitoring
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must provide a carer UI that enables family and carers to check on user activity and receive customizable reports summarizing user behavior.
  - **Citation**: "A carer UI which enables family and carers to check on user activity and receive customizable reports summarizing user behaviour." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Features

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-05
  - **Requirement Title**: Learn User Routine and Behavior
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must learn the user's routine and behavior to adapt its functionalities, such as issuing notifications based on learned patterns.
  - **Citation**: "Learn user’s routine and behaviour which is further decomposed into issue notifications and learn user’s routine and behaviour." (Page 6)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Emotion-Oriented Requirements Modelling

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-06
  - **Requirement Title**: Automate Physical Environment
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must automate aspects of the physical environment, such as heating and cooling, to enhance user comfort.
  - **Citation**: "The goal of this system is to provide reminders (hydration, medication), identify potential security risks (back door has been left open), identify anomalous situations and to automate the physical environment (heating, cooling)." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Features

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-07
  - **Requirement Title**: Issue Notifications for Anomalies
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must issue notifications to relevant parties (e.g., carers or service providers) when anomalies are detected, such as falls or unusual activity patterns.
  - **Citation**: "The emotional goal safe is linked to the goal detect anomaly because elderly people feel safe knowing that in case of an anomaly such as a fall, the system will detect the anomaly and escalate the incident to the service provider and/or carer." (Page 6)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Emotion-Oriented Requirements Modelling

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-08
  - **Requirement Title**: Use Non-Intrusive Sensors
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must utilize non-intrusive motion sensors to monitor user activity without compromising privacy or making users feel old by wearing devices.
  - **Citation**: "This technology was designed as an ambient system using non-intrusive motion sensors to alleviate elder people’s concerns regarding their privacy, and also ‘feeling old’ when they have to put wearables on." (Page 2)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Introduction

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-09
  - **Requirement Title**: Limit Interaction Messages
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must limit interaction messages to essential communications, such as morning messages and medication reminders, to avoid overwhelming users.
  - **Citation**: "To ensure that users felt in control, only two set of messages were included in the system, namely, the morning messages and the medication reminders. The rationale behind this decision was to avoid overwhelming the users with too many messages." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-10
  - **Requirement Title**: Collect Sensor Data for Validation
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must collect data from motion and contact sensors to record activities such as motion, door status, temperature, and humidity for validation and testing of AI components.
  - **Citation**: "Sensor data was collected from the motion and contact sensors installed in the homes. These recorded motion, opening and closing of specific doors, air temperature and humidity." (Page 8)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Trial and SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Evaluation

### Non-Functional Requirements

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-01
  - **Requirement Title**: Privacy Protection
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must ensure user privacy by securing data collected through sensors and restricting access to authorized personnel only.
  - **Citation**: "Similarly, in order to ensure privacy, all the data captured by the sensors were kept within the hub and nobody, except for those authorised by the ethics committee, had access to these data." (Page 7)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-02
  - **Requirement Title**: User Safety Perception
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must enhance the user's perception of safety by ensuring that anomalies are detected and communicated effectively to provide reassurance.
  - **Citation**: "One of our key initial goals identified for SofiHub was to make elderly adults feel safe and reassure them, as well as their relatives, that SofiHub can support them in their day to day activities." (Page 10)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Feeling Safe and Reassured

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-03
  - **Requirement Title**: System Integration into Daily Life
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must be well-integrated into the user's daily life to minimize disruption and negative emotional reactions such as frustration or fear of technology.
  - **Citation**: "While designing SofiHub one of the most important considerations was to ensure that the system is well integrated in the user’s life i.e. to ensure its functional features and quality goals minimize these negative technology reactions." (Page 10)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: System is Integrated in User’s Life

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-04
  - **Requirement Title**: User Control Perception
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must ensure users feel in control of the technology, avoiding perceptions of being controlled by the system.
  - **Citation**: "This shows that overall, the participants felt that they were in control when using SofiHub." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: In Control

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-05
  - **Requirement Title**: Minimize Invasiveness
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must minimize feelings of invasiveness by ensuring that monitoring does not make users feel anxious or uncomfortable.
  - **Citation**: "Findings from interview and questionnaire data shows that overall, participants did not feel that SofiHub was being invasive nor did they feel that their privacy was being violated." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Privacy and Invasiveness

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-06
  - **Requirement Title**: User Comfort with Technology
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must increase user comfort with technology by being easy to use and reducing discomfort or fear associated with new technology.
  - **Citation**: "Felt that SofiHub increased their comfort, and that SofiHub operated smoothly." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Other Views on Using SofiHub

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-07
  - **Requirement Title**: User Confidence in Usage
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must foster user confidence in using the technology, ensuring they do not feel scared or uncertain about its operation.
  - **Citation**: "Were confident in their ability to use it." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Other Views on Using SofiHub

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-08
  - **Requirement Title**: System Reliability
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must operate reliably without frequent disruptions due to power failures or other issues to avoid user frustration or worry.
  - **Citation**: "Users raised concerns about the system being disrupted and the delay in playing the messages." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Emotional Threats

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-09
  - **Requirement Title**: Response Time for Alerts
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must ensure quick response times for generating alerts in emergencies to meet user expectations and enhance trust in the system.
  - **Citation**: "Their only concerns with adopting this technology were linked to (i) cost and (ii) response time to generate an alert in case of an emergency." (Page 13)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Discussion

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-10
  - **Requirement Title**: Cost Effectiveness
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must be cost-effective to encourage adoption by users who may have financial constraints.
  - **Citation**: "Their only concerns with adopting this technology were linked to (i) cost and (ii) response time to generate an alert in case of an emergency." (Page 13)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Discussion

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-11
  - **Requirement Title**: Alleviate Loneliness
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must contribute to reducing feelings of loneliness among users by providing interactions that simulate companionship.
  - **Citation**: "Our evaluation analysis showed that SofiHub helped in alleviating the feeling of loneliness among several participants." (Page 10)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Reduced Loneliness

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-12
  - **Requirement Title**: Feeling Cared About
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must enhance the user's feeling of being cared about through regular interactions and messages that convey concern and support.
  - **Citation**: "Having SofiHub around the house made the elderly person feel cared about as they felt that someone was concerned about them and talked to them on a daily basis." (Page 10)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Reduced Loneliness

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-13
  - **Requirement Title**: User Excitement and Engagement
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must evoke excitement and engagement among users to encourage positive emotional responses and sustained usage.
  - **Citation**: "These findings show that in general, the participants felt excited to have SofiHub installed in their homes." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Other Views on Using SofiHub

- **Role**: Smart Home System (SofiHub)
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-14
  - **Requirement Title**: Smooth Operation
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must operate smoothly without perceived complexity to ensure user satisfaction and ease of use.
  - **Citation**: "Felt that SofiHub operated smoothly." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Other Views on Using SofiHub

  - **Requirement ID**: NF-15
  - **Requirement Title**: Chatty Interaction
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must provide a chatty interaction style to enhance user engagement and emotional connection, such as varying messages or adding night messages.
  - **Citation**: "While the next generation of SofiHub will maintain most of its functional goals, two new emotional goals (‘less lonely’ and ‘cared about’) and a quality goal ‘chatty’ has been included in the refined goal model." (Page 11)
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Implications of Findings on the Next Generation of SofiHub
