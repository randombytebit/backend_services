**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** System
- **Requirement ID:** FR-01
- **Title:** User Authentication Management
- **Description:** The system should provide users with the ability to choose whether to be authenticated or not through the app's settings and allow access using a login or social network account.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1, Requirements 1.1.1.1 and 1.1.1.2]
- **Related Title:** Secure Access Control in Health Apps

**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** User
- **Requirement ID:** FR-02
- **Title:** Personal Data Management
- **Description:** The system should allow users to manage personal information by editing, removing, adding, and sharing personal data in their profile, respecting different countries' specific legislation or regulations regarding user ownership of personal information.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1, Requirements 1.1.2.1 to 1.1.2.4]
- **Related Title:** Privacy-Compliant Health Data Systems

**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** User
- **Requirement ID:** FR-03
- **Title:** Personal Health Record Management
- **Description:** The system should enable users to manage their personal health records by consulting health recommendations, and editing, removing, adding, sharing, importing, exporting, and backing up health record data.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1, Requirements 1.2.1 to 1.2.2.7]
- **Related Title:** Comprehensive Health Record Platforms

**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** System
- **Requirement ID:** FR-04
- **Title:** Social Interaction and Solidarity Promotion
- **Description:** The system should promote interaction among users via social networks and/or online healthcare forums and encourage social solidarity through a rewards system.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 3, Section 3.7.6.2, Requirements 1.1 and 1.2]
- **Related Title:** Community Engagement in Health Apps

**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** System
- **Requirement ID:** FR-05
- **Title:** Multilingual and Cultural Adaptation
- **Description:** The system should be designed for cultural diversity and multilingual use by adapting content to user language preferences, supporting multiple languages, allowing language switching, and using appropriate formats, units of measurement, or currency for an international audience.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 3, Section 3.7.6.2, Requirements 1.3.1 to 1.3.9]
- **Related Title:** Global Health Application Accessibility

**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** System
- **Requirement ID:** FR-06
- **Title:** Environmental Impact Reduction
- **Description:** The system should reduce environmental impact by enabling users to find nearby healthcare centers, facilitating data interchange with EHRs, PHRs, or third parties, and using visual aids like pictures, icons, and graphs to explain content and health data history.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 4, Section 3.7.6.3, Requirements 1.1.1 to 1.2.3]
- **Related Title:** Eco-Friendly Health Service Navigation

**Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** System
- **Requirement ID:** FR-07
- **Title:** Connectivity with IT Resources
- **Description:** The system should connect to other IT resources such as data repositories, drivers, cloud systems, social networks, device features like Bluetooth and NFC, and maps repositories to display locations.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 4, Section 3.7.6.3, Requirements 1.3.1 to 1.3.4]
- **Related Title:** Integrated Health Data Ecosystems

**Non-Functional Requirement**
- **Role:** System
- **Requirement ID:** NFR-01
- **Title:** Technical Sustainability and Energy Efficiency
- **Description:** The system should ensure technical sustainability by adapting to future operating system changes, respecting mobile OS development guidelines, being energy-efficient through features like shutting down in idle mode and supporting multiple profiles on the same device, and providing offline access to app content and data editing capabilities.
- **Citation:** Ouhbi, S., et al. (2018). Sustainability requirements for connected health applications. J Softw Evol Proc, 30:e1922. [Table 5, Section 3.7.6.4, Requirements 1.1.1 to 1.3.3]
- **Related Title:** Long-Term Health App Viability
