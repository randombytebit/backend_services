Title request: medical app
Non-Functional Requirement 
Role: System 
Requirement ID: NFR1 
Requirement Title: Accessibility
Requirement Description: Accessibility is commonly understood as the degree to which the application is available to users, usually taking into account disability measures. Related to technically catering to the right audience using smartphones and tablets for family caregivers and nurses, and adjusting access modes (e.g., with or without health record access) for different stakeholders.
Citation: Page 8, Section 3.2 Stakeholder Interviews
Non-Functional Requirement 
Role: System 
Requirement ID: NFR2 
Requirement Title: Certifiability
Requirement Description: The necessity for the app to be CE-certified as a medical device to be used legally, since software assisting patients in monitoring or preventing diseases and medications is classed as a medical device per European Union directive. Users value certification, especially younger groups, over GP recommendations for older groups.
Citation: Page 7, Section 3.1 Document Study; Page 9, Section 3.3 Vignette-Guided Interviews

Non-Functional Requirement 
Role: System 
Requirement ID: NFR4 
Requirement Title: Privacy
Requirement Description: Compliance with privacy regulations such as the Dutch Data Protection Act for processing personal or medical information, requiring patient approval for transfer and processing by third parties, ensuring privacy is not disproportionately harmed, and favoring a general cause. Users want control over who can access data and are willing to share anonymously or personally if it improves advice, rating it low importance.
