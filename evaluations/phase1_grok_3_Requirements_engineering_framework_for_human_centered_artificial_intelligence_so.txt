Title request: Human-Centered AI Software Systems

Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-01
Requirement Title: Identify User Needs for AI System
Requirement Description: The system must identify and document user needs to ensure the AI-based software addresses specific human-centered requirements, including who the users are, why the system is needed, and what it is used for.
