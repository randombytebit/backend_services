Title request: medical app

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-01
Requirement Title: Accessibility
Requirement Description: The medical app should be accessible to users with diverse physical and psychological abilities, ensuring it can be used by people with a wide range of characteristics, especially considering the diverse needs of medical app users.
Citation: Page 11, Meulendijk et al. /What concerns users of medical apps?

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-02
Requirement Title: Certifiability
Requirement Description: The medical app must be certifiable by recognized authorities to guarantee its quality and reliability, adhering to legal requirements such as CE-certification for medical devices as defined by European Union directives.
Citation: Page 7, Meulendijk et al. /What concerns users of medical apps?

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NF-03
Requirement Title: Portability
Requirement Description: The medical app should support multiple operating systems and devices to ensure it can be transferred from one environment to another, catering to a broad user base.
Citation: Page 8, Meulendijk et al. /What concerns users of medical apps?

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-04
Requirement Title: Privacy
Requirement Description: The medical app must comply with privacy regulations, ensuring that personal and medical information is processed only with user approval and is not disproportionately harmful to the user's privacy, as per the Dutch Data Protection Act.

Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-05
Requirement Title: Safety
Requirement Description: The medical app should provide correct results with the needed degree of precision to safeguard users' well-being, ensuring that the advice or information given does not negatively impact health by delaying diagnoses or advocating incorrect self-medication.
