Title request: Medical App
Non-Functional Requirement
Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-01
Requirement Title: Accessibility
Requirement Description: Accessibility is commonly understood as the degree to which the application is available to users, usually taking into account disability measures. Accessibility, or software’s ability to be “use[d] by people with a wide range of characteristics”, is found explicitly in standards but often understood as an implicit aspect of usability.
Citation: Page 8; Page 9, Section 3.4

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-02
Requirement Title: Certifiability
Requirement Description: The necessity of certifiability, based on a European Union directive which defines that a medical device or aid has to be CE-certified to be used legally, and proposes a definition for what a medical device is. Software that assists patients in monitoring or preventing diseases and medications is classed as a medical device. The degree to which software’s behavior is approved by authorities.
Citation: Page 7, Section 3.1; Page 10, Table 1

Requirement ID: NF-03
Requirement Title: Portability
Requirement Description: Portability refers to the number of operating systems and devices the application supports. The ability of software to be transferred from one environment to another, sometimes referred to as adaptability, installability or compatibility.

Role: User
Requirement ID: NF-04
Requirement Title: Privacy
Requirement Description: Privacy revolves around privacy regulations and how an application that deals with personal or medical information should incorporate those. The processing of any personal information is limited by the Dutch Data Protection Act; both the transfer and processing of information by third parties requires patient approval. The assurance that data are accessible only to those authorized to have access.
