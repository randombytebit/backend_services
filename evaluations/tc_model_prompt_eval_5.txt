Role: System 
Requirement ID: NF-02 
Requirement Title: Certifiability
Requirement Description: The necessity of certifiability is based on a European Union directive, which defines that a medical device or aid has to be CE-certified to be used legally, and proposes a definition for what a medical device is. Software that assists patients in monitoring or preventing diseases and medications is in fact classed as a medical device.

Non-Functional Requirement 
Role: System 
Requirement ID: NF-04 
Requirement Title: Privacy
Requirement Description: Privacy relates to safeguarding users’ well-being when using the application. The processing of any personal information is limited by the Dutch Data Protection Act; both the transfer and processing of information by third parties, entered by patients, will need to be approved by those patients. Apart from getting approval, other conditions that have to be met include the aspect that the patients’ privacy is not disproportionately harmed, and that the process favors a ‘general cause’.
