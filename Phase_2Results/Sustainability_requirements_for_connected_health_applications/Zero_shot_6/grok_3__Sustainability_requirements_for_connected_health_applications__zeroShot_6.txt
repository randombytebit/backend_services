**Non-Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: Developer/User
- **Requirement ID**: NF-01
- **Requirement Title**: Usability for Frequent Use
- **Requirement Description**: The app shall be designed for convenient and frequent use by incorporating visual aids like pictures, icons, and graphs to enhance user understanding and interaction.
- **Citation**: Ouhbi et al., 2018, Table 4, Requirement 1.2
- **Requirement Related Title**: Environmental Impact - Convenient Use

- **Role**: Developer/User
- **Requirement ID**: NF-02
- **Requirement Title**: Cognitive Load Reduction through Visuals
- **Requirement Description**: The app shall minimize cognitive load by using visual elements (pictures, icons, graphs) to explain content and health data history, ensuring users can easily comprehend information.
- **Citation**: Ouhbi et al., 2018, Table 4, Requirements 1.2.1-1.2.3
- **Requirement Related Title**: Environmental Impact - Convenient Use

- **Role**: Developer/User
- **Requirement ID**: NF-03
- **Requirement Title**: Response Time for Energy Efficiency
- **Requirement Description**: The app shall ensure quick response times by shutting down in idle mode to conserve energy, thereby enhancing user experience and device battery life.
- **Citation**: Ouhbi et al., 2018, Table 5, Requirement 1.2.1
- **Requirement Related Title**: Technical Impact - Energy Efficiency

- **Role**: Developer/User
- **Requirement ID**: NF-04
- **Requirement Title**: Accessibility for Multilingual Users
- **Requirement Description**: The app shall be accessible to a diverse user base by supporting multiple languages, adapting content to user language preferences, and providing culturally appropriate formats and terminology.
- **Citation**: Ouhbi et al., 2018, Table 3, Requirements 1.3.1-1.3.9
- **Requirement Related Title**: Social Impact - Cultural Diversity and Multilingual Use

- **Role**: Developer/User
- **Requirement ID**: NF-05
- **Requirement Title**: Usability through Operability Standards
- **Requirement Description**: The app shall adhere to operability standards (e.g., ISO/IEC 25010) to ensure high usability, making it intuitive and easy for users to navigate and interact with the app.
- **Citation**: Ouhbi et al., 2018, Section 3.1
- **Requirement Related Title**: Requirements Specification - Software Quality

- **Role**: Developer/User
- **Requirement ID**: NF-06
- **Requirement Title**: Accessibility for Offline Use
- **Requirement Description**: The app shall ensure accessibility by allowing offline access and data management, catering to users in areas with limited or no Internet connectivity.
- **Citation**: Ouhbi et al., 2018, Table 5, Requirements 1.3.1-1.3.3
- **Requirement Related Title**: Technical Impact - Offline Access
