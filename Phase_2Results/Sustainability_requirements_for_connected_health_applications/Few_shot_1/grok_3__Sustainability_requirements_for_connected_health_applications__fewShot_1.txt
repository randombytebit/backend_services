Below are the extracted Functional (FR) and Non-Functional (NFR) requirements from the provided document titled "Sustainability requirements for connected health applications" by Sofia Ouhbi et al. The requirements are formatted according to the specified rules and cover various dimensions of sustainability for connected health (CH) applications as outlined in the paper.

---

### Functional Requirements (FR)

**Requirement ID: FR-01**  
**Requirement Title: User Authentication and Access Control**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall provide an authentication procedure for users to access the connected health application. Users should have the option to choose whether to be authenticated or not via the app's settings and should be able to access the app using a login or social network account.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.1, Table 2, Requirements for Individual Impact (1.1.1.1, 1.1.1.2)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Blood Donation Apps, Personal Health Record (PHR) Systems, Telehealth Platforms  

**Requirement ID: FR-02**  
**Requirement Title: Personal Data Management**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall allow users to manage their personal information in compliance with different countries' specific legislation or regulations regarding user ownership of personal data. Users should be able to edit, remove, add, and share personal data from their profiles.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.1, Table 2, Requirements for Individual Impact (1.1.2.1 to 1.1.2.4)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Mobile Personal Health Records (mPHRs), Patient Portal Systems, Health Data Sharing Platforms  

**Requirement ID: FR-03**  
**Requirement Title: Personal Health Record Management**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall enable users to manage their personal health records by allowing them to consult health recommendations, edit, remove, add, share, import, export, and back up health record data.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.1, Table 2, Requirements for Individual Impact (1.2.1, 1.2.2.1 to 1.2.2.7)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Chronic Disease Monitoring Apps, Electronic Health Record (EHR) Integration Systems, Health Data Backup Tools  

**Requirement ID: FR-04**  
**Requirement Title: Social Interaction and Community Building**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall promote interaction among users via social networks and/or online healthcare forums and encourage social solidarity through a rewards system to enhance community engagement.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.2, Table 3, Requirements for Social Impact (1.1, 1.2)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Health Support Group Apps, Blood Donation Community Platforms, Patient Engagement Systems  

**Requirement ID: FR-05**  
**Requirement Title: Multilingual and Cultural Adaptation**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall be designed for cultural diversity and multilingual use by adapting content to the user's language preferences, supporting multiple languages, allowing language switching, adapting help sections and terminology, and using appropriate formats for international audiences (e.g., units of measurement, currency, text alignment).  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.2, Table 3, Requirements for Social Impact (1.3.1 to 1.3.9)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Global Telemedicine Apps, International Health Education Platforms, Multilingual Patient Support Systems  

**Requirement ID: FR-06**  
**Requirement Title: Environmental Impact through Reduced Transportation**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall reduce the need for physical transportation by enabling users to find nearby healthcare centers and providing data interchange capabilities with EHRs, PHRs, or third parties (send and receive data).  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.3, Table 4, Requirements for Environmental Impact (1.1.1, 1.1.2.1, 1.1.2.2)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Location-Based Health Service Apps, Remote Consultation Systems, EHR Integration Tools  

**Requirement ID: FR-07**  
**Requirement Title: User-Friendly Content Presentation**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall be convenient for frequent use by employing visual aids such as pictures, icons, and graphs to explain ideas and illustrate health data history.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.3, Table 4, Requirements for Environmental Impact (1.2.1 to 1.2.3)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Health Monitoring Dashboards, Patient Education Apps, Visual Health Data Analytics Tools  

**Requirement ID: FR-08**  
**Requirement Title: Connectivity with IT Resources**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall connect to other IT resources, including data repositories, drivers, cloud systems for backups, social networks, device features like Bluetooth or NFC, and maps repositories to display locations.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.3, Table 4, Requirements for Environmental Impact (1.3.1 to 1.3.4)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Cloud-Based Health Apps, IoT Health Devices, Geolocation Health Service Apps  

**Requirement ID: FR-09**  
**Requirement Title: Offline Access and Data Management**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall allow users to access the app without an internet connection, edit data offline, and save edited data offline for later synchronization.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.4, Table 5, Requirements for Technical Impact (1.3, 1.3.1 to 1.3.3)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Rural Health Monitoring Apps, Offline Patient Record Systems, Disaster Response Health Apps

### Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)

**Requirement ID: NFR-01**  
**Requirement Title: Privacy and Security Compliance**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall respect the security and privacy of users by implementing robust authentication procedures and ensuring compliance with international and local regulations regarding personal data protection.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.1, Table 2, Requirements for Individual Impact (1.1)  
**Requirement Related Title:** HIPAA-Compliant Health Apps, GDPR-Compliant Telehealth Systems, Secure Patient Data Platforms  

**Requirement ID: NFR-02**  
**Requirement Title: Usability for Personal Health and Well-Being**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall promote personal health and well-being by providing an intuitive and user-friendly interface for consulting health recommendations and managing personal health records.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.1, Table 2, Requirements for Individual Impact (1.2)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Elderly Health Monitoring Apps, Wellness Coaching Platforms, User-Centric Health Apps  

**Requirement ID: NFR-03**  
**Requirement Title: Social Sustainability through Community Engagement**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall have a positive social impact by fostering interaction and solidarity among users through features like social networks, forums, and rewards systems, while ensuring cultural and linguistic adaptability.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.2, Table 3, Requirements for Social Impact (1)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Community Health Apps, Social Support Platforms for Chronic Illness, Multicultural Health Education Systems  

**Requirement ID: NFR-04**  
**Requirement Title: Environmental Sustainability**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall have a positive environmental impact by reducing the need for physical transportation through location-based services and data interchange capabilities, and by promoting frequent use through user-friendly design.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.3, Table 4, Requirements for Environmental Impact (1)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Green Telehealth Solutions, Remote Health Monitoring Systems, Eco-Friendly Health Apps  

**Requirement ID: NFR-05**  
**Requirement Title: Technical Sustainability and Longevity**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall ensure technical sustainability by easily adapting to future operating system changes, adhering to mobile OS development guidelines, and being platform-adapted to ensure longevity and non-obsolescence.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.4, Table 5, Requirements for Technical Impact (1.1, 1.1.1, 1.1.2)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Cross-Platform Health Apps, Long-Term Support Telemedicine Systems, OS-Compatible Health Solutions  

**Requirement ID: NFR-06**  
**Requirement Title: Energy Efficiency**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall be energy-efficient by shutting down in idle mode and supporting multiple user profiles on the same device to minimize resource consumption.  
**Citation:** Section 3.7.6.4, Table 5, Requirements for Technical Impact (1.2, 1.2.1, 1.2.2)  
**Requirement Related Title:** Green Mobile Health Apps, Battery-Optimized Telehealth Tools, Energy-Saving Patient Monitoring Systems  

**Requirement ID: NFR-07**  
**Requirement Title: Standards Compliance for Quality and Interoperability**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description:** The system shall comply with relevant standards such as ISO/IEC 25010 for software quality, ISO 9241-151 for ergonomics, ISO/TR 18307 for interoperability, and other health informatics standards (e.g., ISO/HL7 27931, ISO 21090) to ensure quality, usability, and data exchange compatibility.  
**Citation:** Section 3.1, Requirements Specification  
**Requirement Related Title:** Standards-Compliant EHR Systems, Interoperable Telehealth Platforms, Quality-Assured Health Apps  

---

These requirements are derived from the detailed catalog (SCH-CAT) presented in the paper and cover the individual, social, environmental, and technical dimensions of sustainability for connected health applications. They are intended to be reusable and adaptable for various CH app contexts, as demonstrated in the paper's application example with the Blood Donor+ app.
