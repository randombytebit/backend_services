Requirement ID: FR-01  
Requirement Title: User Authentication and Personal Data Management  
Requirement Description: The system shall provide an authentication procedure allowing users to opt-in or opt-out via settings, access the app using login credentials or social network accounts, and manage personal information in compliance with regional legislation, including editing, adding, removing, and sharing profile data.  
Citation: Table 2 (Section 3.7.6.1 Individual impact, requirements 1.1 and 1.1.2)  
Related Title: Personal Health Record (PHR) Management Apps, Patient Portal Access Systems  

Requirement ID: FR-02  
Requirement Title: Personal Health Record Management  
Requirement Description: The system shall enable users to manage their personal health records by consulting health recommendations, editing, adding, removing, sharing, importing, exporting, and backing up health data to promote personal health and well-being.  
Citation: Table 2 (Section 3.7.6.1 Individual impact, requirements 1.2 and 1.2.2)  
Related Title: Chronic Disease Self-Management Apps, Mobile Fitness and Wellness Trackers  

Requirement ID: FR-03  
Requirement Title: Social Interaction and Solidarity Promotion  
Requirement Description: The system shall facilitate user interactions through integration with social networks or online healthcare forums and promote social solidarity via a rewards system to encourage community building and collaborative health support.  
Citation: Table 3 (Section 3.7.6.2 Social impact, requirements 1.1 and 1.2)  
Related Title: Online Health Support Communities, Peer-to-Peer Chronic Illness Networks  

Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Requirement Title: Cultural Diversity and Multilingual Support  
Requirement Description: The system shall support cultural diversity and multilingual use by adapting content, online help, terminology, text alignment, formats, units, and advertisements to user language preferences, with clear presentation of supported languages and options to switch languages or select media languages at multiple points.  
Citation: Table 3 (Section 3.7.6.2 Social impact, requirement 1.3 and sub-requirements)  
Related Title: Global Telehealth Platforms, International Patient Education Apps  

Requirement ID: NFR-02  
Requirement Title: Environmental Impact Reduction and Convenience  
Requirement Description: The system shall minimize environmental impact by enabling users to locate nearby healthcare centers, interchange data with EHRs, PHRs, or third parties (including sending and receiving data), and enhance usability through pictures, icons, graphs for data visualization, while connecting to IT resources like backups, social networks, device features (Bluetooth/NFC), and maps for location display to promote frequent, efficient use and reduce transportation needs.  
Citation: Table 4 (Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental impact, requirements 1.1, 1.2, and 1.3)  
Related Title: Eco-Friendly Telemedicine Services, Location-Based Health Navigation Apps  

Requirement ID: NFR-03  
Requirement Title: Technical Adaptability and Energy Efficiency  
Requirement Description: The system shall ensure longevity and efficiency by adapting to future OS changes through compliance with mobile OS guidelines and platform adaptations, being energy-efficient (e.g., shutting down in idle mode and supporting multiple profiles on the same device), and providing offline access for viewing, editing, and saving data without internet connectivity.  
Citation: Table 5 (Section 3.7.6.4 Technical impact, requirements 1.1, 1.2, and 1.3)  
Related Title: Long-Term Wearable Health Devices, Sustainable IoT Health Ecosystems
