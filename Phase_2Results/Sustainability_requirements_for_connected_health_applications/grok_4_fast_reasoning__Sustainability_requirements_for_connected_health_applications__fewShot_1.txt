### Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-01  
**Title:** Authentication Procedure  
**Description:** The system shall implement an authentication procedure to ensure user security and privacy, allowing users to access the app via login or social network account, with options to enable or disable authentication in settings.  
**Citation:** Table 2 (Section 3.7.6.1 Individual impact, 1.1.1 and sub-requirements), Page 5  
**Related Title:** Blood Donation Management Apps, Personal Health Record (PHR) Systems, Telemedicine Patient Portals  

### Functional Requirement
**Role:** User  
**Requirement ID:** FR-02  
**Title:** Personal Data Management  
**Description:** The user shall be able to manage personal information in the profile, including editing, adding, removing, and sharing personal data, while respecting country-specific legislation on data ownership.  
**Citation:** Table 2 (Section 3.7.6.1 Individual impact, 1.1.2 and sub-requirements), Page 5  
**Related Title:** Mobile Personal Health Records (mPHRs), Chronic Disease Self-Management Apps, Fitness Tracking Applications  

### Functional Requirement
**Role:** User  
**Requirement ID:** FR-03  
**Title:** Health Record Management  
**Description:** The user shall be able to manage their personal health record by consulting health recommendations, editing, adding, removing, sharing, importing, exporting, and backing up data in the health record.  
**Citation:** Table 2 (Section 3.7.6.1 Individual impact, 1.2 and sub-requirements), Page 5  
**Related Title:** Cardiovascular Patient Monitoring Apps, Pregnancy Tracking Systems, Cancer Survivor Support Tools  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-01  
**Title:** Positive Individual Impact  
**Description:** The system shall ensure a positive individual impact by respecting user security, privacy, and promoting personal health and well-being through accessible health recommendations and record management features.  
**Citation:** Table 2 (Section 3.7.6.1 Individual impact, main requirement 1), Page 5  
**Related Title:** Wellness and Mental Health Apps, Elderly Care Telehealth Platforms, Disability Assistance Software  

### Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-04  
**Title:** Social Interaction Promotion  
**Description:** The system shall promote interaction among users via integration with social networks and/or online healthcare forums to foster community building and social solidarity.  
**Citation:** Table 3 (Section 3.7.6.2 Social impact, 1.1 and 1.2), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Online Health Support Communities, Peer-to-Peer Chronic Illness Networks, Social Wellness Challenges Apps  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-02  
**Title:** Cultural Diversity and Multilingual Support  
**Description:** The system shall be designed for cultural diversity and multilingual use, adapting content, help sections, terminology, text alignment, formats, units, and advertisements to user language preferences and international audiences to avoid cultural discrepancies.  
**Citation:** Table 3 (Section 3.7.6.2 Social impact, 1.3 and sub-requirements), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Global mHealth Apps for Migrants, International Telecare Services, Cross-Cultural Disease Prevention Tools  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-03  
**Title:** Positive Social Impact  
**Description:** The system shall have a positive social impact by promoting user interactions, solidarity through rewards, and cultural inclusivity to preserve social capital and community services.  
**Citation:** Table 3 (Section 3.7.6.2 Social impact, main requirement 1), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Community Health Initiatives, Solidarity-Based Donation Platforms, Social Equity Health Apps  

### Functional Requirement
**Role:** User  
**Requirement ID:** FR-05  
**Title:** Healthcare Center Location and Data Interchange  
**Description:** The user shall be able to find nearby healthcare centers and interchange data with Electronic Health Records (EHRs), Personal Health Records (PHRs), or third parties by sending and receiving data to reduce transportation needs.  
**Citation:** Table 4 (Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental impact, 1.1 and sub-requirements), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Location-Based Telehealth Services, Environmental Health Mapping Apps, Sustainable Transport Health Integrations  

### Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-06  
**Title:** Visual and Connectivity Features for Convenience  
**Description:** The system shall use pictures, icons, and graphs to explain ideas and illustrate health data history for frequent use, and connect to IT resources like data repositories, social networks, device features (e.g., Bluetooth/NFC), and maps for backups and location display.  
**Citation:** Table 4 (Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental impact, 1.2 and 1.3 sub-requirements), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Eco-Friendly mHealth Interfaces, Connected Device Ecosystems, Visual Data Analytics in Health Apps  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-04  
**Title:** Positive Environmental Impact  
**Description:** The system shall have a positive environmental impact by reducing transportation through location services and data interchange, enhancing convenience with visual aids, and minimizing resource use via efficient IT connections.  
**Citation:** Table 4 (Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental impact, main requirement 1), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Green Telemedicine Systems, Resource-Efficient Wearable Health Tech, Sustainable Digital Health Ecosystems  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-05  
**Title:** OS Adaptability and Energy Efficiency  
**Description:** The system shall easily adapt to future operating system changes by respecting mobile OS guidelines and platform adaptations, and be energy-efficient by shutting down in idle mode and supporting multiple profiles on the same device.  
**Citation:** Table 5 (Section 3.7.6.4 Technical impact, 1.1 and 1.2 sub-requirements), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Long-Term Mobile Health Software, Energy-Aware IoT Health Devices, Cross-Platform Telehealth Apps  

### Functional Requirement
**Role:** User  
**Requirement ID:** FR-07  
**Title:** Offline Access Capabilities  
**Description:** The user shall have offline access to the app, including accessing content without internet connection, editing data offline, and saving edited data locally.  
**Citation:** Table 5 (Section 3.7.6.4 Technical impact, 1.3 and sub-requirements), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Remote Area Health Monitoring Apps, Offline PHR Systems, Disaster Response mHealth Tools  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-06  
**Title:** Positive Technical Impact  
**Description:** The system shall have a positive technical impact by ensuring longevity through OS adaptability, energy efficiency, and offline access to support evolution with changing conditions and non-obsolescence.  
**Citation:** Table 5 (Section 3.7.6.4 Technical impact, main requirement 1), Page 6  
**Related Title:** Maintainable eHealth Platforms, Scalable Connected Care Systems, Future-Proof Medical Software  

### Non-Functional Requirement
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-07  
**Title:** Overall Sustainability Compliance  
**Description:** The system shall conform to sustainability requirements across individual, social, environmental, and technical dimensions, derived from ISO/IEC 25010, ISO 9241-151, and other e-health standards, to ensure durability, adoption, and positive impacts without economic inherent constraints.  
**Citation:** Section 3 (SCH-CAT development), Tables 1-5 (Sustainability subsections 3.7.6), Pages 4-6  
**Related Title:** Sustainable mHealth Ecosystems, Regulatory-Compliant Telehealth Apps, Green Software for Healthcare
