Below are the extracted Functional (FR) and Non-Functional (NFR) requirements from the provided document titled "Sustainability requirements for connected health applications" by Sofia Ouhbi et al. The requirements are formatted according to the specified rules and cover various dimensions of sustainability for connected health (CH) applications as outlined in the paper.

---

### Functional Requirements (FR)

**Requirement ID: FR-01**  
**Requirement Title: User Authentication and Access Control**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall provide an authentication procedure for users to access the connected health application. Users should have the option to choose whether to be authenticated or not via the app's settings and should be able to access the app using a login or social network account.**  
**Citation: Page 5, Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1 Individual Impact, Requirements 1.1.1.1 and 1.1.1.2**  
**Requirement Related Title: Blood Donation Apps, Personal Health Record (PHR) Systems, Telehealth Platforms**

**Requirement ID: FR-02**  
**Requirement Title: Personal Data Management**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall allow users to manage their personal information in compliance with different countries' specific legislation or regulations regarding user ownership of personal information. Users should be able to edit, remove, add, and share personal data from their profiles.**  
**Citation: Page 5, Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1 Individual Impact, Requirements 1.1.2.1 to 1.1.2.4**  
**Requirement Related Title: Mobile Personal Health Records (mPHR), Patient Portal Systems, Health Data Sharing Platforms**

**Requirement ID: FR-03**  
**Requirement Title: Personal Health Record Management**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall enable users to manage their personal health records by allowing them to edit, remove, add, share, import, export, and back up health record data. Additionally, users should be able to consult health recommendations through the app.**  
**Citation: Page 5, Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1 Individual Impact, Requirements 1.2.1 to 1.2.2.7**  
**Requirement Related Title: Chronic Disease Monitoring Apps, Electronic Health Record (EHR) Integration Systems, Wellness Tracking Apps**

**Requirement ID: FR-04**  
**Requirement Title: Social Interaction and Community Building**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall promote interaction among users via social networks and/or online healthcare forums and encourage social solidarity through a rewards system to enhance community engagement.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 3, Section 3.7.6.2 Social Impact, Requirements 1.1 and 1.2**  
**Requirement Related Title: Health Support Group Apps, Blood Donation Community Platforms, Patient Advocacy Networks**

**Requirement ID: FR-05**  
**Requirement Title: Multilingual and Cultural Adaptation**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall be designed for cultural diversity and multilingual use by adapting content to the user's language preferences, supporting multiple languages, allowing language switching, adapting help sections and terminology, and using appropriate formats for international audiences (e.g., units of measurement, currency, text alignment).**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 3, Section 3.7.6.2 Social Impact, Requirements 1.3.1 to 1.3.9**  
**Requirement Related Title: Global Telemedicine Apps, International Health Education Platforms, Cross-Cultural Health Apps**

**Requirement ID: FR-06**  
**Requirement Title: Environmental Impact through Reduced Transportation**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall reduce the need for transportation by enabling users to find nearby healthcare centers and providing data interchange capabilities with EHRs, PHRs, or third parties (send and receive data).**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 4, Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental Impact, Requirements 1.1.1 to 1.1.2.2**  
**Requirement Related Title: Location-Based Health Service Apps, Remote Consultation Platforms, EHR Integration Tools**

**Requirement ID: FR-07**  
**Requirement Title: User-Friendly Content Presentation**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall be convenient for frequent use by using pictures, icons, and graphs to explain ideas and illustrate health data history, enhancing user comprehension and engagement.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 4, Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental Impact, Requirements 1.2.1 to 1.2.3**  
**Requirement Related Title: Health Education Apps, Patient Monitoring Dashboards, Fitness Tracking Apps**

**Requirement ID: FR-08**  
**Requirement Title: Connectivity with IT Resources**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall connect to other IT resources, including data repositories, drivers, cloud systems for backups, social networks, device features like Bluetooth or NFC, and maps repositories to display locations.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 4, Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental Impact, Requirements 1.3.1 to 1.3.4**  
**Requirement Related Title: Smart Health Devices, Cloud-Based Health Apps, Geolocation Health Services**

**Requirement ID: FR-09**  
**Requirement Title: Offline Access and Data Management**  
**Role: System and User**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall allow users to access the app without an internet connection, edit data offline, and save edited data offline to ensure usability in varied connectivity scenarios.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 5, Section 3.7.6.4 Technical Impact, Requirements 1.3 to 1.3.3**  
**Requirement Related Title: Rural Health Monitoring Apps, Emergency Health Apps, Offline Medical Reference Tools**

---

### Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)

**Requirement ID: NFR-01**  
**Requirement Title: Privacy and Security Compliance**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall respect the security and privacy of users by implementing robust authentication procedures and ensuring compliance with international and local regulations regarding personal data protection.**  
**Citation: Page 5, Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1 Individual Impact, Requirement 1.1**  
**Requirement Related Title: Secure Health Data Platforms, HIPAA-Compliant Health Apps, GDPR-Compliant Medical Systems**

**Requirement ID: NFR-02**  
**Requirement Title: Usability for Individual Well-Being**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall promote personal health and well-being by providing an intuitive and user-friendly interface that supports health recommendations and personal health record management, ensuring a positive individual impact.**  
**Citation: Page 5, Table 2, Section 3.7.6.1 Individual Impact, Requirement 1.2**  
**Requirement Related Title: Patient Self-Management Apps, Wellness Coaching Apps, Mental Health Support Tools**

**Requirement ID: NFR-03**  
**Requirement Title: Social Sustainability and Inclusivity**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall have a positive social impact by promoting interaction, solidarity, and inclusivity through features that support cultural diversity, multilingual content, and community engagement, ensuring accessibility to diverse user groups.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 3, Section 3.7.6.2 Social Impact, Requirement 1**  
**Requirement Related Title: Community Health Apps, Multilingual Telehealth Systems, Social Health Networks**

**Requirement ID: NFR-04**  
**Requirement Title: Environmental Sustainability**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall have a positive environmental impact by reducing the need for physical transportation through location-based services and data interchange capabilities, and by promoting digital interactions over physical resources.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 4, Section 3.7.6.3 Environmental Impact, Requirement 1**  
**Requirement Related Title: Green Telemedicine Solutions, Eco-Friendly Health Apps, Remote Care Platforms**

**Requirement ID: NFR-05**  
**Requirement Title: Technical Sustainability and Longevity**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall ensure technical sustainability by easily adapting to future operating system changes, adhering to mobile OS development guidelines, and being platform-compatible to ensure longevity and non-obsolescence.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 5, Section 3.7.6.4 Technical Impact, Requirements 1.1 to 1.1.2**  
**Requirement Related Title: Long-Term Health Apps, Cross-Platform Medical Software, Future-Proof Telehealth Systems**

**Requirement ID: NFR-06**  
**Requirement Title: Energy Efficiency**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall be energy-efficient by shutting down in idle mode and supporting multiple user profiles on the same device to minimize resource consumption and extend device battery life.**  
**Citation: Page 6, Table 5, Section 3.7.6.4 Technical Impact, Requirements 1.2 to 1.2.2**  
**Requirement Related Title: Green Mobile Health Apps, Energy-Saving Medical Devices, Sustainable Health Software**

**Requirement ID: NFR-07**  
**Requirement Title: Standards Compliance for Quality and Interoperability**  
**Role: System**  
**Requirement Description: The system shall comply with relevant standards such as ISO/IEC 25010 for software quality, ISO 9241-151 for human-system interaction, ISO/TR 18307 for interoperability, and other health informatics standards to ensure quality, usability, and compatibility in health information exchange.**  
**Citation: Page 3, Section 3.1 Requirements Specification**  
**Requirement Related Title: Standards-Based Health Apps, Interoperable Telehealth Systems, Regulatory-Compliant Medical Software**

---

These requirements are derived from the detailed catalog (SCH-CAT) presented in the paper and cover the individual, social, environmental, and technical dimensions of sustainability for connected health applications. They are intended to be reusable and adaptable for various CH app contexts, as demonstrated in the paper's application example with the Blood Donor+ app.
