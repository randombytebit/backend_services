REQUIREMENT REPORT

**Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: FR-01
- **Requirement Title**: Bias Detection Modules
- **Requirement Description**: The system must include modules specifically designed to detect and mitigate biases in data processing and decision-making algorithms to ensure fair outcomes across diverse user groups.
- **Citation**: Vergilio, T. and Ramachandran, M. (2019). Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1, pp. 833-840.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Bias Mitigation in Data Processing

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: FR-02
- **Requirement Title**: Explainability Dashboards
- **Requirement Description**: The system must provide user-friendly dashboards that offer detailed explanations of algorithmic decisions and data processing steps to enhance user understanding and trust.
- **Citation**: Vergilio, T. and Ramachandran, M. (2019). Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1, pp. 833-840.
- **Requirement Related Title**: User Trust through Explainability

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: FR-03
- **Requirement Title**: Audit Trail Generation
- **Requirement Description**: The system must automatically generate comprehensive audit trails for all data processing activities and algorithmic decisions to support accountability and compliance with regulatory standards.
- **Citation**: Vergilio, T. and Ramachandran, M. (2019). Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1, pp. 833-840.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Accountability through Audit Trails
