Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-01
Title: Fault Tolerance
Description: The system should continue to operate should one or more nodes fail, recovering gracefully with minimal repercussions on the user experience, through mechanisms such as node independence, checkpointing, and redundancy.
Citation: Vergilio, T and Ramachandran, M (2019) Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1. pp. 833-840. DOI: https://doi.org/10.5220/0006825408330840
Related Title: Fault-Tolerant ML Inference Systems, High-Availability AI Platforms

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-02
Title: Processing Guarantees
Description: The system should provide processing guarantees such as exactly once, at least once, and at most once to ensure reliable data processing, minimizing duplication or loss in high-stakes AI operations.
Citation: Vergilio, T and Ramachandran, M (2019) Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1. pp. 833-840. DOI: https://doi.org/10.5220/0006825408330840
Related Title: Reliable Stream Processing ML Systems, Data Integrity in AI Pipelines
