**REQUIREMENT REPORT**

**Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: MLOps Architect
- **Requirement ID**: FR-01
- **Requirement Title**: Batch Data Processing Orchestration
- **Requirement Description**: The system must support the orchestration of batch data processing for large volumes of finite data, such as data archived in distributed file systems or databases, with capabilities to handle hundreds of petabytes as seen in architectures like Facebook and Twitter.
- **Citation**: Vergilio, T. and Ramachandran, M. (2019). Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1, pp. 833-840.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Batch Data Processing Capability

- **Role**: MLOps Architect
- **Requirement ID**: FR-02
- **Requirement Title**: Stream Data Processing Orchestration

- **Citation**: Vergilio, T. and Ramachandran, M. (2019). Non-functional requirements for real world big data systems: an investigation of big data architectures at Facebook, Twitter and Netflix. Proceedings of the 13th International Conference on Software Technologies, 1, pp. 833-840.
