Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-01  
Title: Demographic Data Documentation  
Description: System should document demographic data including identity (first name and surname) and insurance (insurance name and number).  
Citation: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 1.

Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-02  
Title: Health Record Management  
Description: System should manage health records including vital sign documentation (blood pressure, heart rate), kidney documentation (blood sugar), fitness documentation (weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin documentation (condition of skin), history documentation (disease history, family history, allergy, medications history, vaccination, surgeries, smoking), present history documentation (symptoms and conditions, X-ray, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), documentation of inpatient treatment history (date of admission and discharge, hospital name, chief complaint, summary), and medication documentation (name, dose, type, instruction, side effects).  
Citation: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 1.  
Related Title: Electronic Health Record System for Remote Patient Monitoring  

Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-03  
Title: General Features of User Interface  
Description: System should provide general UI features including reminders (appointment, preventive care), display (patient’s self-declaration about his health status, consultant identity: name and surname and profile picture, curriculum vitae: expertise and consulting field, type of communication: phone call, text, video call, and real-time, appointment: consultation date, time, duration, and waiting time for response), service selection (choosing consultation day, time, and type of insurance), reporting (providing weekly/monthly reports on the patient’s treatment process, providing weekly/monthly reports of vital signs and medical history), support (documentation of communication ways: tagging capability in VCMA and frequently asked questions, Q and A ability: discussion forum with others (patients and consultants)), and help/tutorial (text guide: help, training inside VCMA: working with it, about us, terms and conditions of use).  
Citation: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 1.  
Related Title: User Interface for Virtual Consultation Scheduling  

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Usability  
Description: System should ensure usability through settings including creating a personal profile for the user (consultant-patient), microphone, camera, measurement units selection (selection of measurement units like centimeters and meters for height), video call with other participants (making a video call with other connected individuals), selecting conversation starter, and log out.  
Citation: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 2.
