Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-01
Title: Demographic Data Documentation
Description: System should allow documentation of identity (first name and surname) and insurance (name and number).

Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-02
Title: Health Record Management
Description: System should facilitate documentation of vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate), kidney (blood sugar), fitness (weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin (condition of skin), history (disease history, family history, allergy, medications history, vaccination, surgeries, smoking), present history (symptoms and conditions, X-ray, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), inpatient treatment history (date of admission and discharge, hospital name, chief complaint, summary), and medication (name, dose, type, instruction, side effects).
Citation: Parsaei, Z. et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. Table 1.
Related Title: Electronic Health Record Systems in Mobile Apps

Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-03
Title: General Features of User Interface
Description: System should provide reminders (appointment, preventive care), display (patient’s self-declaration about his health status, consultant identity: name and surname and profile picture, curriculum vitae: expertise and consulting field, type of communication: phone call, text, video call, and real-time, appointment: consultation date, time, duration, and waiting time for response), service selection (choosing consultation day, time, and type of insurance), reporting (providing weekly/monthly reports on the patient’s treatment process, providing weekly/monthly reports of vital signs and medical history), support (documentation of communication ways: tagging capability in VCMA and frequently asked questions, Q and A ability: discussion forum with others (patients and consultants)), and help/tutorial (text guide: help, training inside VCMA: working with it, about us, terms and conditions of use).
Citation: Parsaei, Z. et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. Table 1.
