Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-02  
Title: Health Record Management  
Description: System should manage comprehensive health records including vital signs documentation (blood pressure, heart rate), kidney documentation (blood sugar), fitness documentation (weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin documentation (condition of skin), history documentation (disease history, family history, allergy, medications history, vaccination, surgeries, smoking), present history documentation (symptoms and conditions, X-ray, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), documentation of inpatient treatment history (date of admission and discharge, hospital name, chief complaint, summary), and medication documentation (name, dose, type, instruction, side effects).  
Citation: Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications.[cite: Table 1, Page 4]  
Related Title: Electronic Health Record Systems[cite: Table 1, Page 4]  

Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-03  
Title: General Features of User Interface  
Description: System should provide general UI features including reminders (appointment, preventive care), display (patient’s self-declaration about health status, consultant identity with name/surname/profile picture, curriculum vitae with expertise/consulting field, type of communication such as phone call/text/video call/real-time, appointment details including date/time/duration/waiting time), service selection (choosing consultation day/time/type of insurance), reporting (weekly/monthly reports on patient’s treatment process, vital signs, and medical history), support (documentation of communication ways with tagging capability/FAQs, Q&A/discussion forum with patients/consultants), and help/tutorial (text guide/help, training inside VCMA, about us, terms and conditions of use).  
Citation: Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications.[cite: Table 1, Page 4]

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Usability  
Description: System should support usability features including settings for creating personal profiles (consultant-patient), microphone/camera access, measurement units selection (e.g., centimeters/meters for height), video calls with other participants, selecting conversation starters, and log out functionality; these ensure intuitive operation and verifiable user interactions for trust in virtual clinic outputs.
