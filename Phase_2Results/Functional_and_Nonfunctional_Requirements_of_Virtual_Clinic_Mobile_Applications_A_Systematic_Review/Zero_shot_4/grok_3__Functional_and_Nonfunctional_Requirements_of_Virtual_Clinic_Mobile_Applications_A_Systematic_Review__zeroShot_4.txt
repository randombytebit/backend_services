REQUIREMENT REPORT

**Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: FR-01
- **Requirement Title**: Bias Detection Modules
- **Requirement Description**: The system must include modules to detect and mitigate biases in AI algorithms, ensuring equitable outcomes across diverse user groups by identifying potential discriminatory patterns in data processing and decision-making.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Health Record Management

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: FR-02
- **Requirement Title**: Explainability Dashboards
- **Requirement Description**: The system must provide user-friendly dashboards that offer clear explanations of AI-driven decisions, enabling users to understand the rationale behind outcomes and fostering trust in the system.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
- **Requirement Related Title**: General Features of User Interface (UI)

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: FR-03
- **Requirement Title**: Audit Trail Generation
- **Requirement Description**: The system must generate detailed audit trails for all AI operations, documenting data inputs, processing steps, and decision outputs to support accountability and compliance with regulatory standards.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Reporting Features in UI

**Non-Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: NF-01
- **Requirement Title**: Fairness Metrics
- **Requirement Description**: The system must implement fairness metrics to evaluate and ensure equitable treatment across different demographic groups, minimizing disparities in AI outcomes.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Usability

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: NF-02
- **Requirement Title**: Transparency Levels
- **Requirement Description**: The system must maintain high transparency levels by providing accessible information about AI processes, data usage, and decision-making criteria to users and stakeholders.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Accessibility

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: NF-03
- **Requirement Title**: Interpretability
- **Requirement Description**: The system must ensure interpretability by presenting AI decisions and processes in a manner that is easily understandable to non-technical users, supporting informed decision-making.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
- **Requirement Related Title**: Compatibility

- **Role**: AI Governance Lead
- **Requirement ID**: NF-04
- **Requirement Title**: Ethical Alignment
- **Requirement Description**: The system must align with ethical guidelines and principles, ensuring that AI operations adhere to societal values, privacy standards, and moral considerations in healthcare delivery.
- **Citation**: Parsaei, Z., et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, 2024, Article ID 7800321.
