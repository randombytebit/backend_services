**Functional Requirement**  
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-01  
**Title:** Demographic Data Documentation  
**Description:** The system should allow users to document demographic data, including identity information such as first name and surname, as well as insurance details like insurance name and number.  
**Citation:** Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. *International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications*, 2024, Article ID 7800321, Table 1.  
**Related Title:** Patient Registration System for Telemedicine Platforms

**Functional Requirement**  
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-02  
**Title:** Health Record Management  
**Description:** The system should enable comprehensive health record management, including documentation of vital signs (e.g., blood pressure, heart rate), kidney-related data (e.g., blood sugar), fitness data (e.g., weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin conditions, medical history (e.g., disease history, family history, allergies, medications, vaccinations, surgeries, smoking), present history (e.g., symptoms, X-rays, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), inpatient treatment history (e.g., admission/discharge dates, hospital name, chief complaint), and medication details (e.g., name, dose, type, instructions, side effects).  
**Citation:** Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. *International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications*, 2024, Article ID 7800321, Table 1.  
**Related Title:** Electronic Health Record Integration for Virtual Care

**Functional Requirement**  
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-03  
**Title:** General Features of User Interface (UI)  
**Description:** The system should provide general UI features such as reminders for appointments and preventive care, display of patient self-declarations and consultant identity (e.g., name, surname, profile picture, expertise), communication options (e.g., phone call, text, video call, real-time), appointment scheduling (e.g., date, time, duration, waiting time), service selection (e.g., consultation day, time, insurance type), reporting (e.g., weekly/monthly reports on treatment process and vital signs), support features (e.g., tagging, FAQs, discussion forums), and help/tutorials (e.g., text guides, in-app training, terms and conditions).  
**Citation:** Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. *International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications*, 2024, Article ID 7800321, Table 1.  
**Related Title:** Telehealth Appointment Scheduling Interface

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-01  
**Title:** Usability  
**Description:** The system should ensure usability by allowing users to create personal profiles, configure settings (e.g., microphone, camera, measurement units like centimeters or meters for height), initiate video calls with other participants, select conversation starters, and log out of the application.  
**Citation:** Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. *International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications*, 2024, Article ID 7800321, Table 2.  
**Related Title:** User-Friendly Telemedicine App Design

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** NFR-02  
**Title:** Accessibility  
**Description:** The system should provide accessibility features such as the ability to save and mark appointments, reminders, notes, recorded voices, photos, and messages; send feedback on consultant performance; select text from a default list; and share individual lessons, health record information with doctors, and patient-consultant data exchange.  
**Citation:** Parsaei, Z., Jangi, M., Tahmasebian, S., & Ehteshami, A. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. *International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications*, 2024, Article ID 7800321, Table 2.
