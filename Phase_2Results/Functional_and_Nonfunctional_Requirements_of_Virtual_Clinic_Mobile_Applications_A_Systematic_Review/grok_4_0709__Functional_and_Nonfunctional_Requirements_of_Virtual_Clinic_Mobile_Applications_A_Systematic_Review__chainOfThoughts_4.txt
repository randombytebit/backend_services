Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-06  
Title: Testability  
Description: System should support systematic validation of its outputs through techniques such as the Cochrane Collaboration’s tool, including random sequence generation, allocation concealment, blinding of participants and personnel, blinding of outcome assessment, incomplete outcome data, selective reporting, and other sources of bias, independently of using external processes to improve validation itself.
