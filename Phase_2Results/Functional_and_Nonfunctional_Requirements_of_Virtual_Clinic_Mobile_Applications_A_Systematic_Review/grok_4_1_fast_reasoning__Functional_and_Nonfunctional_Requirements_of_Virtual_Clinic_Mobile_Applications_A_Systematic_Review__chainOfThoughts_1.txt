Functional Requirement  
Role: VCMA System  
Requirement ID: FR-01  
Title: Demographic Data Documentation  
Description: The system must document demographic data including identity (first name and surname) and insurance (insurance name and number).

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: VCMA System  
Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Usability  
Description: The system must support usability features including creating a personal profile for the user (consultant-patient), microphone, camera, measurement units selection (e.g., centimeters and meters for height), video call with other participants (making a video call with other connected individuals), selecting conversation starter, and log out.  
Citation: Table 2 (Page 5), Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications.  
Related Title: User-Friendly Telemedicine Interfaces, Accessible Remote Consultation Apps, Intuitive mHealth Self-Management Tools

Functional Requirement  
Role: VCMA System  
Requirement ID: FR-02  
Title: Health Record Management  
Description: The system must manage health records including vital sign documentation (blood pressure, heart rate), kidney documentation (blood sugar), fitness documentation (weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin documentation (condition of skin), history documentation (disease history, family history, allergy, medications history, vaccination, surgeries, smoking), present history documentation (symptoms and conditions, X-ray, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), documentation of inpatient treatment history (date of admission and discharge, hospital name, chief complaint, summary), and medication documentation (name, dose, type, instruction, side effects).  
Citation: Table 1 (Page 4), Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications.  
Related Title: Chronic Disease Remote Monitoring Apps, Electronic Health Record Portals for Outpatients, Personalized Patient History Trackers  

Functional Requirement  
Role: VCMA System  
Requirement ID: FR-03  
Title: General Features of User Interface (UI)  
Description: The system must provide general UI features including reminders (appointment, preventive care), display (patient’s self-declaration about his health status, consultant identity: name and surname and profile picture, curriculum vitae: expertise and consulting field, type of communication: phone call, text, video call, and real-time, appointment: consultation date, time, duration, and waiting time for response), service selection (choosing consultation day, time, and type of insurance), reporting (providing weekly/monthly reports on the patient’s treatment process, providing weekly/monthly reports of vital signs and medical history), support (documentation of communication ways: tagging capability in VCMA and frequently asked questions, Q and A ability: discussion forum with others (patients and consultants)), and help/tutorial (text guide: help, training inside VCMA: working with it, about us, terms and conditions of use).  
Citation: Table 1 (Page 4), Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications.  
Related Title: Interactive Virtual Consultation Dashboards, Patient Engagement UI in mHealth Apps, Multimodal Telehealth Communication Interfaces
