Requirement ID: FR-01  
Requirement Title: Demographic Data Documentation  
Requirement Description: The system must enable users to document and store patient demographic information, including identity details such as first name and surname, and insurance information such as insurance name and number, to facilitate patient identification and billing processes during virtual consultations.  
Citation: Table 1 (A. Demographic data documentation), Page 4  
Requirement Related Title: Patient Onboarding in Telemedicine Platforms, Insurance Integration in Remote Healthcare Apps  

Requirement ID: FR-02  
Requirement Title: Comprehensive Health Record Management  
Requirement Description: The system must support detailed documentation and management of patient health records, including vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate), blood sugar levels, fitness metrics (weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin condition, medical histories (disease, family, allergy, medications, vaccination, surgeries, smoking), present history (symptoms, conditions, X-rays, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), inpatient treatment history (admission/discharge dates, hospital name, chief complaint, summary), and medication details (name, dose, type, instructions, side effects), to enable monitoring, decision-making, and self-management.  
Citation: Table 1 (B. Health record), Page 4  
Requirement Related Title: Chronic Disease Self-Management Apps, Electronic Health Record (EHR) Integration in Virtual Clinics, Postoperative Follow-Up Portals  

Requirement ID: FR-03  
Requirement Title: User Interface and Consultation Features  
Requirement Description: The system must provide general UI features including reminders for appointments and preventive care, displays for patient self-declarations, consultant profiles (name, surname, picture, expertise, consulting field), communication types (phone call, text, video call, real-time), appointment details (date, time, duration, waiting time), service selection (consultation day, time, insurance type), weekly/monthly reporting on treatment processes, vital signs, and medical history, support via tagging, FAQs, and Q&A discussion forums, and help/tutorials (text guides, in-app training, about us, terms and conditions).  
Citation: Table 1 (C. General features of UI), Page 4  
Requirement Related Title: Virtual Consultation Scheduling Systems, Patient-Provider Communication Dashboards, mHealth Reminder and Reporting Tools  

Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Requirement Title: Usability and User Settings  
Requirement Description: The system must ensure high usability through features like creating personal profiles for consultants and patients, access to microphone and camera, selection of measurement units (e.g., cm/m for height), video calls with multiple participants, conversation starters, and secure log-out options, to support intuitive and effective user interactions.  
Citation: Table 2 (A. Usability), Page 5  
Requirement Related Title: Cross-Platform Telehealth User Interfaces, Multilingual Virtual Care Apps, Elderly-Friendly mHealth Platforms

Citation: Table 2 (B. Accessibility), Page 5
