Functional Requirement  
Role: VCMA System  
Requirement ID: FR-02  
Title: Health Record Management  
Description: Vital sign documentation: Blood pressure, Heart rate. Kidney documentation: Blood sugar. Fitness documentation: Weight, Exercise, Sleep, Nutrition. Skin documentation: Condition of skin. History documentation: Disease history, Family history, Allergy, Medications history, Vaccination, Surgeries, Smoking. Present history documentation: Symptoms and conditions, X-ray, Tests, Treatments, Prescriptions, Patient training. Documentation of inpatient treatment history: Date of admission and discharge, Hospital name, Chief complaint, Summary. Medication documentation: Name, Dose, Type, Instruction, Side effects.

Functional Requirement  
Role: VCMA System  
Requirement ID: FR-03  
Title: General Features of User Interface (UI)  
Description: Reminders: Appointment, Preventive care. Display: Patient’s self-declaration about his health status, Consultant identity: name and surname and profile picture, Curriculum vitae: expertise and consulting field, Type of communication: phone call, text, video call, and real-time, Appointment: consultation date, time, duration, and waiting time for response. Service selection: Choosing consultation day, time, and type of insurance. Reporting: Providing weekly/monthly reports on the patient’s treatment process, Providing weekly/monthly reports of vital signs and medical history. Support: Documentation of communication ways: tagging capability in VCMA and frequently asked questions, Q and A ability: discussion forum with others (patients and consultants). Help/tutorial: Text guide: help, Training inside VCMA: working with it, About us, Terms and conditions of use.
