Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-01  
Title: Demographic Data Documentation  
Description: The system must enable documentation of patient demographic data, including identity details such as first name and surname, and insurance information such as insurance name and number, to support user registration and verification in virtual clinic services.  
Citation: Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 1.  
Related Title: Patient Onboarding in Telehealth Platforms, Insurance Enrollment in Remote Care Apps  

Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-02  
Title: Health Record Management  
Description: The system must facilitate comprehensive health record management, including documentation of vital signs (e.g., blood pressure, heart rate), kidney-related metrics (e.g., blood sugar), fitness data (e.g., weight, exercise, sleep, nutrition), skin condition, medical history (e.g., disease, family, allergy, medications, vaccination, surgeries, smoking), present history (e.g., symptoms, conditions, X-ray, tests, treatments, prescriptions, patient training), inpatient treatment history (e.g., admission/discharge dates, hospital name, chief complaint, summary), and medication details (e.g., name, dose, type, instruction, side effects), to enable monitoring and clinical decision-making.  
Citation: Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 1.  
Related Title: Electronic Health Records in Mobile Telemedicine, Chronic Disease Tracking in Virtual Care Systems  

Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: FR-03  
Title: General Features of User Interface  
Description: The system must provide general UI features, including reminders for appointments and preventive care; displays for patient's self-declared health status, consultant identity (name, surname, profile picture), curriculum vitae (expertise, consulting field), communication types (phone call, text, video call, real-time), and appointment details (date, time, duration, waiting time); service selection for consultation day, time, and insurance type; reporting of weekly/monthly treatment processes, vital signs, and medical history; support via communication documentation (tagging, FAQs), Q&A forums with patients and consultants; and help/tutorials (text guides, in-app training, about us, terms and conditions), to enhance user interaction and service delivery.  
Citation: Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 1.  
Related Title: UI Design for Teleconsultation Apps, Appointment Scheduling in Remote Healthcare Interfaces  

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Usability  
Description: The system must ensure usability through features such as creating personal profiles for users (consultants and patients), enabling microphone and camera access, allowing selection of measurement units (e.g., centimeters/meters for height), supporting video calls with connected participants, providing conversation starters, and including a log-out function, to make the application intuitive and user-friendly.  
Citation: Parsaei et al. (2024). Functional and Nonfunctional Requirements of Virtual Clinic Mobile Applications: A Systematic Review. International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Table 2.  
Related Title: User Profile Management in mHealth Apps, Video Conferencing Usability in Virtual Consultations
