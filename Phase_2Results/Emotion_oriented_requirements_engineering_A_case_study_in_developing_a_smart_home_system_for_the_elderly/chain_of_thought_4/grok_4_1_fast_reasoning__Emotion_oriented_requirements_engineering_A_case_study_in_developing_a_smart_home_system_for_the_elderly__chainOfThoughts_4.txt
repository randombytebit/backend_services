Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Testability  
Description: System should support systematic validation of its AI outputs through ground-truth data established via daily checklists cross-checked against sensor data during weekly face-to-face interviews.  
Citation: Curumsing, M.K. et al. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147, 215–229.[cite: Page 8, Section 4.3.1]  
Related Title: Smart Home AI Anomaly Detection Systems, Elderly Monitoring Platforms[cite: Page 8, Section 4.3.1]  

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-02  
Title: AI Accuracy Testing  
Description: System should enable testing of the accuracy of its sensors and AI components via local storage of sensor data (motion, contact, temperature, humidity) for periodic retrieval and analysis.  
Citation: Curumsing, M.K. et al. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147, 215–229.[cite: Page 8, Section 4.3.1]  
Related Title: IoT Sensor Validation in Aged-Care Systems, Runtime AI Behavior Learning[cite: Page 8, Section 4.3.1]
