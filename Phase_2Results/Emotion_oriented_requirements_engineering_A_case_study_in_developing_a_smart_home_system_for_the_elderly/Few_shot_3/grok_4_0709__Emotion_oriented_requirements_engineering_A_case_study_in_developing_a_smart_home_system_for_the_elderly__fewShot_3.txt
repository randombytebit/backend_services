Requirement ID: NFR-01  
Title: Fairness in Elderly Monitoring  
Description: System should not enforce unintended biases towards vulnerable groups such as the elderly, and should define and measure fairness formally (e.g., via statistical parity or individual fairness) in a context-specific manner to ensure equitable monitoring and assistance.  
Citation: Section 1. Introduction, where software engineers fail to consider emotional needs leading to unhappy end-users, and evidence suggests user emotions determine technology acceptance, particularly for domestic systems targeting elderly.
