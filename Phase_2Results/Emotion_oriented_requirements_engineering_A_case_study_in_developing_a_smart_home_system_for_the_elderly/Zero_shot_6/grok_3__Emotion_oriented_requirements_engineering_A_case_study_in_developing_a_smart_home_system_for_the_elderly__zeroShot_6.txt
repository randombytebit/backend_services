REQUIREMENT REPORT

**Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-01
  - **Requirement Title**: Non-Intrusive Motion Sensors
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must include non-intrusive motion sensors to monitor user activity without violating privacy or making users feel old, supporting independent living by detecting anomalies such as falls.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 216
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Privacy-Preserving Monitoring

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-02
  - **Requirement Title**: User-Specific Reminders
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must provide user-specific reminders for hydration, medication, appointments, and special events, triggered by user activity to integrate seamlessly into daily routines.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Routine Integration

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-03
  - **Requirement Title**: Anomaly Detection Alerts
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must issue alerts to users and carers when anomalous behavior is detected, such as spending an abnormal amount of time in bed, to ensure safety and reassurance.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Safety Alerts

- **Role**: Carer/Family Member
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-04
  - **Requirement Title**: Carer User Interface
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must provide a carer UI that allows family and carers to check on user activity and receive customizable reports summarizing user behavior for monitoring and reassurance.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Remote Monitoring

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-05
  - **Requirement Title**: Limited Interaction Messages
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must limit interaction messages to essential communications such as morning messages and medication reminders to avoid overwhelming users and ensure they feel in control.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Controlled Interaction

- **Role**: Researcher/Evaluator
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-06
  - **Requirement Title**: Emotion-Oriented Questionnaire Tool
  - **Requirement Description**: The system evaluation must include an emotion-oriented questionnaire tool with simplified terms and a Likert scale to assess users’ emotional responses to the system at different trial intervals.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 222
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Emotional Feedback Collection

- **Role**: Researcher/Evaluator
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-07
  - **Requirement Title**: Interview Data Collection
  - **Requirement Description**: The system evaluation must include structured interviews conducted at pre-trial, mid-trial, post-trial, and post-uninstallation stages to capture users’ emotional experiences and perceptions of the technology.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 222
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Qualitative Emotional Insights

- **Role**: Researcher/Evaluator
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-08
  - **Requirement Title**: Daily Checklists for Validation
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must provide daily checklists for users to record activities, which are cross-checked with sensor data during weekly interviews to validate the accuracy of the AI component and sensor data.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 222
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Data Validation Tool

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-09
  - **Requirement Title**: Sensor Data Collection Interface
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must include an interface for collecting data from motion and contact sensors to record user activities, environmental conditions like temperature and humidity, ensuring local storage for privacy.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 222
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Activity Monitoring Interface

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-10
  - **Requirement Title**: Automated Environment Control
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must automate physical environment controls such as heating and cooling to enhance user comfort and integrate into daily life without requiring manual intervention.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Comfort Automation

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: FR-11
  - **Requirement Title**: Security Risk Notifications
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must notify users of potential security risks, such as a back door left open, to enhance feelings of safety and security within the home environment.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Security Alerts

**Non-Functional Requirements**

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-01
  - **Requirement Title**: Usability for Elderly Users
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must be designed to be simple and intuitive for elderly users, minimizing complexity and frustration to ensure it integrates well into their daily lives and reduces negative emotional reactions.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 224
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Ease of Use

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-02
  - **Requirement Title**: Cognitive Load Minimization
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must minimize cognitive load by limiting the frequency and complexity of interactions, such as messages, to prevent overwhelming users and maintain their sense of control.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Reduced Mental Effort

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-03
  - **Requirement Title**: Response Time for Alerts
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must ensure rapid response times for generating alerts in emergency situations to meet user expectations for safety and reassurance, addressing concerns about delays.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 227
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Timely Emergency Response

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-04
  - **Requirement Title**: Accessibility for Elderly
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must be accessible to elderly users with varying levels of physical and mental capacity, ensuring that interfaces and interactions are understandable and usable without causing discomfort or fear.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 224
  - **Requirement Related Title**: User-Friendly Design

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-05
  - **Requirement Title**: Privacy Assurance
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must ensure high levels of privacy by securing data collected through sensors and restricting access to authorized personnel only, alleviating user concerns about invasiveness and data breaches.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 221
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Data Security

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-06
  - **Requirement Title**: Emotional Goal Integration
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must prioritize emotional goals such as feeling safe, independent, and cared about, ensuring these are reflected in design to enhance user acceptance and adoption.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 223
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Emotional Design Focus

- **Role**: Elderly User
  - **Requirement ID**: NF-07
  - **Requirement Title**: System Reliability
  - **Requirement Description**: The system must operate reliably without frequent disruptions due to power failures or other issues, minimizing user concerns about system dependability and ensuring consistent emotional support.
  - **Citation**: Curumsing et al. (2019), p. 225
  - **Requirement Related Title**: Dependable Operation
