REQUIREMENT REPORT
- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: FR-01
- Requirement Title: Support Independent Living
- Requirement Description: The system shall support independent living for elderly people through passive monitoring, reminders, anomaly detection, and assistance to enable continued living at home.
- Citation: Page 5 (Goal model); Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Support Independent Living

- Role: System
- Requirement ID: FR-02
- Requirement Title: Learn User’s Routine and Behaviour
- Requirement Description: The system shall use built-in AI to learn and adapt to the user's daily routine and behaviour from sensor data.
- Citation: Page 5 (Goal model); Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Learn User’s Routine and Behaviour

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: FR-03
- Requirement Title: Issue Notifications and Reminders
- Requirement Description: The system shall issue user-specific notifications and reminders for hydration, medication, appointments, and special events, triggered by user activity or learned routine.
- Citation: Page 5 (Goal model); Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Issue Notifications

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: FR-04
- Requirement Title: Detect Anomaly
- Requirement Description: The system shall detect anomalous situations such as prolonged time in bed, falls, irregular bathroom visits, or security risks like back doors left open.
- Citation: Page 5 (Goal model); Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Detect Anomaly

- Role: Family Member
- Requirement ID: FR-05
- Requirement Title: Issue Alerts to Carers
- Requirement Description: The system shall issue alerts to family members, carers, or service providers upon detection of anomalies or emergencies.
- Citation: Page 5 (Goal model); Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Emergency Assistance

- Role: Family Member
- Requirement ID: FR-06
- Requirement Title: Carer User Interface
- Requirement Description: The system shall provide a UI for family members and carers to check user activity and receive customizable reports summarizing behaviour.
- Citation: Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Carer UI

- Role: Maintenance Personnel
- Requirement ID: FR-07
- Requirement Title: Sensor Data Collection
- Requirement Description: The system shall collect data from motion sensors, contact sensors (doors), temperature sensors, and humidity sensors.
- Citation: Page 8 Section 4.3.1
- Requirement Related Title: Sensing

- Role: System
- Requirement ID: FR-08
- Requirement Title: Local Data Storage
- Requirement Description: The system shall store sensor data locally within the SofiHub device.
- Citation: Page 8 Section 4.3.1; Page 7 Section 4.2
- Requirement Related Title: Passive Monitoring

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: FR-09
- Requirement Title: Voice Message Playback
- Requirement Description: The system shall play voice messages such as morning greetings and reminders to interact with the user.
- Citation: Page 10 Section 5.2.1; Page 11 Section 5.2.3
- Requirement Related Title: Morning Messages

- Role: System
- Requirement ID: FR-10
- Requirement Title: Automate Physical Environment
- Requirement Description: The system shall automate the physical home environment such as heating and cooling based on sensor data.
- Citation: Page 7 Section 4.1
- Requirement Related Title: Automate Environment

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-01
- Requirement Title: Privacy Secured
- Requirement Description: The system shall secure user data locally with no unauthorized access and protection against hacking to ensure users feel their privacy is maintained.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal); Page 7 Section 4.2
- Requirement Related Title: Privacy Secured

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-02
- Requirement Title: Feel Integrated
- Requirement Description: The system shall integrate seamlessly into the user's daily life without obtrusion or complexity to avoid frustration.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal); Page 11 Section 5.2.3
- Requirement Related Title: Integrated

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-03
- Requirement Title: Feel Comfortable
- Requirement Description: The system shall be easy to use and non-threatening to make elderly users comfortable with the technology.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal); Page 11 Section 5.2.6
- Requirement Related Title: Comfortable

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-04
- Requirement Title: Feel In Control
- Requirement Description: The system shall allow users to maintain control over their lives and the system, with limited and non-overwhelming interactions.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal); Page 11 Section 5.2.4
- Requirement Related Title: In Control

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-05
- Requirement Title: Feel Safe
- Requirement Description: The system shall provide monitoring and anomaly detection to make users feel safe in their home.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal); Page 10 Section 5.2.2
- Requirement Related Title: Safe

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-06
- Requirement Title: Feel Empowered
- Requirement Description: The system shall build user confidence and empowerment through reliable technology support.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal)
- Requirement Related Title: Empowered

- Role: Family Member
- Requirement ID: NF-07
- Requirement Title: Feel Reassured
- Requirement Description: The system shall provide visibility into elderly wellbeing to reassure family members and carers.
- Citation: Page 5 (Emotional goal); Page 10 Section 5.2.2
- Requirement Related Title: Reassured

- Role: Cared-for Ageing Person
- Requirement ID: NF-08
- Requirement Title: Non-Intrusive Design
- Requirement Description: The system shall use passive, non-wearable sensors to avoid invasion of privacy or feeling of being monitored.
- Citation: Page 2 Section 2.1; Page 7 Section 4.2
- Requirement Related Title: Non-Intrusive

- Role: All
- Requirement ID: NF-09
- Requirement Title: Availability and Reliability
- Requirement Description: The system shall maintain high availability and handle disruptions like power failures without impacting core functions.
- Citation: Page 12 Section 5.2.7 (emotional threats)
- Requirement Related Title: Reliability
