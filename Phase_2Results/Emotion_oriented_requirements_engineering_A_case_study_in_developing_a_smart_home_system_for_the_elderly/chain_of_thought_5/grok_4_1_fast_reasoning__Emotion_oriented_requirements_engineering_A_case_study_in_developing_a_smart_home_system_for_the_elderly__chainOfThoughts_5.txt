Requirement ID: FR-01  
Title: Passive Monitoring  
Description: System comprises a range of devices and software for sensing, interaction, passive monitoring, and emergency assistance. Designed as an ambient system using non-intrusive motion sensors to alleviate elder people’s concerns regarding their privacy, and also ‘feeling old’ when they have to put wearables on.

Non-Functional Requirement  
Role: System  
Requirement ID: NFR-02  
Title: Feel Safe  
Description: Knowing that someone or the system can detect an anomaly (for example in case they had a fall) made the older person feel safe. The emotional goal safe is linked to the goal detect anomaly because elderly people feel safe knowing that in case of an anomaly such as a fall, the system will detect the anomaly and escalate the incident to the service provider and/or carer.  
Citation: Page 8, Section 4.2 (SofiHub emotion-oriented RE); Page 6, Section 3.2.1 (Goal model checks).  
Related Title: Safety Assurance in Ambient Assisted Living
