### Functional Requirements (FR)

**Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: FR-01  
- **Title**: Support Independent Living  
- **Description**: The system must support independent living for elderly users by providing functionalities such as passive monitoring through non-intrusive sensors, issuing reminders for hydration and medication, and detecting anomalous behaviors (e.g., prolonged time in bed or irregular bathroom visits). The system should learn the user’s routine and behavior to provide tailored support without making the user feel monitored or dependent.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.1 (SofiHub Features), Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering)  
- **Related Title**: Elderly Assisted Living Systems, Smart Home Automation for Seniors, Independent Living Support Platforms  

**Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: FR-02  
- **Title**: Issue User-Specific Reminders  
- **Description**: The system must issue user-specific reminders for appointments, special events, hydration, and medication, triggered by user activity or predefined schedules. These reminders should be limited in frequency (e.g., morning messages and medication reminders) to avoid overwhelming the user.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.1 (SofiHub Features), Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering)  
- **Related Title**: Personal Health Reminder Apps, Elderly Care Notification Systems, Smart Home Reminder Tools  

**Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: FR-03  
- **Title**: Detect and Alert on Anomalous Behavior  
- **Description**: The system must detect anomalous behaviors (e.g., spending abnormal time in bed or irregular patterns in movement) using sensors and issue alerts to carers or service providers to ensure timely intervention.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.1 (SofiHub Features), Section 5.2.2 (Feeling Safe and Reassured)  
- **Related Title**: Fall Detection Systems, Elderly Monitoring Platforms, Emergency Response Smart Systems  

**Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: FR-04  
- **Title**: Carer Interface for Monitoring and Reports  
- **Description**: The system must provide a carer interface that allows family members or professional carers to monitor user activity remotely and receive customizable reports summarizing the elderly user’s behavior and well-being.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.1 (SofiHub Features)  
- **Related Title**: Remote Caregiver Monitoring Apps, Family Health Tracking Systems, Elderly Care Dashboard Solutions  

**Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: FR-05  
- **Title**: Automate Physical Environment  
- **Description**: The system must automate aspects of the physical environment, such as heating and cooling, to enhance user comfort and safety based on sensor data (e.g., air temperature and humidity).  
- **Citation**: Section 4.1 (SofiHub Features)  
- **Related Title**: Smart Home Climate Control Systems, Elderly Comfort Automation Tools, IoT Environmental Adjustment Platforms  

**Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: FR-06  
- **Title**: Issue Varied Interaction Messages  
- **Description**: The system must issue varied interaction messages (e.g., morning greetings, night messages) to engage users and reduce feelings of loneliness, ensuring the tone and content are friendly and non-obtrusive.  
- **Citation**: Section 5.2.1 (Reduced Loneliness), Section 5.3 (Implications of Findings on the Next Generation of SofiHub)  
- **Related Title**: Social Companion Smart Systems, Elderly Engagement Apps, Voice-Based Interaction Platforms

### Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-01  
- **Title**: Privacy and Data Security  
- **Description**: The system must ensure the privacy of user data by securing all sensor-collected information within the hub, restricting access to authorized personnel only, and preventing unauthorized access or hacking of sensitive data. Users should feel comfortable and not anxious about being monitored.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering), Section 5.2.5 (Privacy and Invasiveness)  
- **Related Title**: IoT Privacy Protection Systems, Elderly Data Security Platforms, Smart Home Privacy Solutions  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-02  
- **Title**: User Feeling of Independence  
- **Description**: The system must be designed to ensure elderly users feel independent by minimizing intrusive monitoring and avoiding features that make them feel controlled or dependent on others. The system should support their desire to live as they please without constant oversight.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering), Section 5.2.1 (Reduced Loneliness), Section 5.2.4 (In Control)  
- **Related Title**: Elderly Autonomy Support Systems, Non-Intrusive Smart Home Solutions, Independent Living Enhancement Tools  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-03  
- **Title**: User Feeling of Safety and Reassurance  
- **Description**: The system must make elderly users and their carers feel safe and reassured by providing reliable detection of anomalies and timely alerts in case of emergencies, ensuring users feel supported in their daily activities.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering), Section 5.2.2 (Feeling Safe and Reassured)  
- **Related Title**: Elderly Safety Assurance Systems, Emergency Response Smart Homes, Reassurance Monitoring Platforms  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-04  
- **Title**: System Integration into User’s Life  
- **Description**: The system must be seamlessly integrated into the daily life of elderly users, ensuring it does not feel like a nuisance or complex technology. It should become part of their routine, minimizing frustration and enhancing positive interactions (e.g., waiting for morning messages).  
- **Citation**: Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering), Section 5.2.3 (System is Integrated in User’s Life)  
- **Related Title**: User-Friendly Smart Home Systems, Elderly Routine Integration Tools, Non-Disruptive Assistive Technologies  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-05  
- **Title**: User Feeling of Control  
- **Description**: The system must ensure elderly users feel in control of their environment and interactions with the technology, avoiding features or behaviors that make them feel controlled by the system (e.g., limiting message frequency to prevent overwhelm).  
- **Citation**: Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering), Section 5.2.4 (In Control)  
- **Related Title**: User-Controlled Smart Home Systems, Elderly Autonomy Interfaces, Non-Overbearing Assistive Technologies  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-06  
- **Title**: Reduction of Loneliness and Feeling Cared About  
- **Description**: The system must address emotional goals of reducing loneliness and making users feel cared about by providing engaging interactions (e.g., personalized messages) that simulate companionship and show concern for the user’s well-being.  
- **Citation**: Section 5.2.1 (Reduced Loneliness), Section 5.3 (Implications of Findings on the Next Generation of SofiHub)  
- **Related Title**: Social Isolation Mitigation Systems, Elderly Emotional Support Platforms, Companion Smart Home Solutions  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-07  
- **Title**: User Comfort and Confidence with Technology  
- **Description**: The system must be designed to make elderly users feel comfortable and confident in using the technology, minimizing fear or frustration associated with complexity, and ensuring smooth operation to enhance user experience.  
- **Citation**: Section 4.2 (SofiHub Emotion-Oriented Requirements Engineering), Section 5.2.6 (Other Views on Using SofiHub)  
- **Related Title**: Elderly-Friendly Technology Interfaces, User Confidence Building Systems, Simplified Smart Home Solutions  

**Non-Functional Requirement**  
- **Role**: System  
- **Requirement ID**: NFR-08  
- **Title**: Reliability and Minimal Disruption  
- **Description**: The system must operate reliably with minimal disruptions (e.g., due to power failures or delays in messages), ensuring users do not feel worried or frustrated by system malfunctions or perceive that they have upset or confused the system.  
- **Citation**: Section 5.2.7 (Emotional Threats)  
- **Related Title**: High-Reliability Smart Home Systems, Elderly Stress-Free Technology Platforms, Robust IoT Solutions for Seniors
