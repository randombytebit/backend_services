Non-Functional Requirement
Role: SofiHub System
Requirement ID: NFR-01
Title: User Safety Assurance
Description: The system must detect anomalous situations, such as falls or irregular activities, and escalate incidents to service providers or carers to ensure elderly users feel safe in their homes.
Citation: Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly, Maheswaree Kissoon Curumsing et al., The Journal of Systems & Software 147 (2019) 215–229.
