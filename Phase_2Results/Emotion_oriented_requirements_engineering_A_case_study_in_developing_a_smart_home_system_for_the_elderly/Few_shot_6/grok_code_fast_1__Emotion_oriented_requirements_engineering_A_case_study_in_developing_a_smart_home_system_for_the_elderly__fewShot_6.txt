Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-01
Title: Sensor Data Validation
Description: System should validate sensor data by cross-checking against user-provided checklists to establish ground-truth and verify the accuracy of sensors and the AI component.
Citation: Kissoon Curumsing, M., Fernando, N., Abdelrazek, M., Vasa, R., Mouzakis, K., Grundy, J. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147, 215–229.

Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-02
Title: AI Output Verification
Description: System should use ground-truth data from user checklists and interviews to verify the results of the AI component, including learning user routines and detecting anomalies.

Title: Accuracy Measurement

Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-02
Title: Risk Assessment Calculation
Description: System should calculate risks by detecting anomalous behavior (e.g., irregular bathroom visits or abnormal time spent in bed) and escalating incidents to service providers or carers to assess and mitigate safety risks.
