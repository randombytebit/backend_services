Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: FR-01
Title: Risk/Value Estimation
Description: System should detect anomaly such as a fall and escalate the incident to the service provider and/or carer.
Citation: Curumsing, M.K. et al. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147, 215–229.[cite: Section 3.2.1]

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-01
Title: Testability
Description: System should support systematic validation of its ML outputs through techniques such as establishing ground-truth to verify the results of its AI component and to validate the sensor data.[cite: Section 4.3.1]
Citation: Curumsing, M.K. et al. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147, 215–229.[cite: Section 4.3.1]

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-02
