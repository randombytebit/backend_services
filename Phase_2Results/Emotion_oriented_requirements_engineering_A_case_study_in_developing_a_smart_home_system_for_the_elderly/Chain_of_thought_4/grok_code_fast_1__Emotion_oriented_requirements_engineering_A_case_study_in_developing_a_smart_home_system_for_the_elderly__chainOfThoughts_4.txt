Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-06
Title: Testability
Description: System should support systematic validation of its AI outputs through cross-checking sensor data with user daily checklists to establish ground-truth for AI predictions, such as learning user routines and detecting anomalies.
Citation: Kissoon Curumsing, M., Fernando, N., Abdelrazek, M., Vasa, R., Mouzakis, K., Grundy, J. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147 (2019) 215–229.
Related Title: Elderly Smart Home Systems, AI Behavior Learning Systems

Non-Functional Requirement
Role: System
Requirement ID: NFR-07
Title: Reliability
Description: System should estimate the confidence and trustworthiness of individual AI predictions at runtime, such as anomaly detection for elderly safety, to ensure reliable support for independence and care.
Citation: Kissoon Curumsing, M., Fernando, N., Abdelrazek, M., Vasa, R., Mouzakis, K., Grundy, J. (2019). Emotion-oriented requirements engineering: A case study in developing a smart home system for the elderly. The Journal of Systems & Software 147 (2019) 215–229.
Related Title: Medical Domain ML Applications, Elderly Care AI Systems
