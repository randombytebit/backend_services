Below are the functional and non-functional requirements derived from the provided text concerning the SofiHub smart home system for the elderly. Each requirement is grounded in the text, with citations to specific sections or pages, and includes a unique related title for contextual application.

**Functional Requirement**  
**Role:** System  
**Requirement ID:** FR-01  
**Title:** Support Independent Living  
**Description:** The system must support independent living for elderly users by providing assistive services such as reminders for hydration and medication, identifying security risks (e.g., open doors), detecting anomalous situations (e.g., prolonged time in bed), and automating the physical environment (e.g., heating and cooling). This functionality aims to enable elderly individuals to live in their own homes with minimal external assistance.  
**Citation:** Curumsing et al., The Journal of Systems & Software 147 (2019), Section 4.1, p. 221  
**Related Title:** Elderly Assistive Smart Home Technology
